Services

Listed below you will find a wide variety of services I provide. Mix and match services to give you and your adventure group the best experience. Contact my office for specific prices based on the services you would like to include in your trip.

Pack In

With the Pack In service I will bring every thing needed for the trip. This includes food, hiking or camping equipment, and much more.

Itinerary

When recieving the Itinerary service I will plan the entire trip as well as oversee from afar. I will not accompany you on the trip but I will be on call the duration of the trip.

Luxury

The Luxury service will provide you with the best hotels, restaraunts and tourist destinations that your desired location has to offer.

Local Aid

The Local Aid service incorporates the local communities in your respective trip and is lead by one of my world renowned partners.

Classic Package

The Classic Package is our most popular choice. I accompany you on your trip for a total of 14 days. You leave the adventure up to me. Its a surprise!

Extended Stay

The Extended Stay Service is a trip with an estimated itinerary of more that 20 days.
